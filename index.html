How To Play

  • Use the up, down, right and left arrow keys to move the ball.
  • The goal of the game is to fit the ball through the space between the two walls without touching either of them.
  • If you touch either of the walls, you lose the game.
  • If you make it throught the space between the walls without touching either of them, then you get a point.
  • The game continues forever until you touch a wall
